Style Engine · dials

Every slider writes a --hd-* CSS variable 0–100. This panel is the seed of the future macro UI.

One dial → many params (wobble, rotation, sketchiness, boil, grain), exactly like an Ableton rack macro.

Motion respects prefers-reduced-motion. Toggle it in your OS to see boil & self-draw freeze.

04 · boil animation

discrete baseFrequency stepping

05 · grain

fractalNoise paper overlay

A fixed full-page grain overlay at 5–15% opacity (Grain dial), multiply-blended over cream paper. Turn Grain up to see it bite.

The grain sits above everything as a multiply overlay. Raise the Grain dial.
06 · self-drawing lines

stroke-dashoffset + getTotalLength()

Path length measured with getTotalLength(); the line draws itself. Duration from the Boil-speed dial. Instant under reduced-motion.

07 · Rough.js shapes

seeded sketchy shapes

Rough.js with roughness from the Sketchiness dial, bowing, and a STABLE-per-element seed (stable on reflow, different between shapes). Re-render on dial change.

08 · rough-notation

annotations, used sparingly

Reserve 3–5 per page for the words that matter most: a deadline, a key number, a warning. Over-use and the eye stops trusting them.

09 · risograph poster (composed)

flat inks · multiply · misregistration · halftone

2–3 flat inks, each on a multiply layer, offset 1–3px (Sketchiness dial) so overlaps mix and edges misregister. Halftone dot grid underneath.
